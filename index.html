Campus Life

School library

a new libraray was made in 2019

In September 2019, phase three of our ambitious plans to upgrade our facilities was completed when our new state-of-the-art library building opened. The building also includes art studios, maths classrooms, a pastoral suite and an assembly space.

School sport

There are many sport facilities here

On-site, the school has a state-of-the-art artificial turf pitch, rugby pitch and several play areas. Off-site facilities include a football pitch, three rugby pitches and a Cricket Ground which is a five minute walk from the school. We also use many other city-based facilities for sports such as football, tennis, indoor climbing, squash, hockey and athletics.

Science and Technology

There are science and technology facilites

The school has a large Science and Technology Centre which houses 15 specialist science laboratories for Biology, Chemistry and Physics, as well as two design and technology laboratories and a teaching kitchen.
